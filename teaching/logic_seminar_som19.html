Logic and Verification Seminar (Summer Semester 2019)

The aim of the seminar is to read specific topics (from original articles, possibly with assistance of textbooks and online materials) in the field of logic and verification including the following possible topics, to name a few and in the broadest possible sense:
  • Semantics of imperative programming languages
  • Algorithmic problems in the field of verification
  • Operationalization of formal specification techniques
  • SMT (Satisfiability Modulo Theories) solving and decision procedures
Advanced bachelor and masters students of TUK are welcome.

Meetings

  • Kickoff meeting: 19 April 22 April (13:30 - 15:00) at 34-421 (or a seminar room nearby)

Assessments

Assessments are based on presentation and productions of written materials (slides/reports). Attendance of the kickoff meeting and at least five other presentations (not including own presentation) is required.
